---
title: Being alive and being modern
---

<!--more-->

Spanish writer [Javier Pérez Andújar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Javier_P%C3%A9rez_And%C3%BAjar) in his weekly segment on ["A Vivir que son dos días"](https://cadenaser.com/cadena-ser/a-vivir-que-son-dos-dias/):

> La gente confunde ser moderno con estar vivo, pero la mayoría de las veces una y otra cosa son todo lo contrario, pues quienes quieren ser muy modernos, en vez de vivir, se desviven.

Which more or less translates to:

> People confuse being modern with being alive, but most of the time the two are complete opposites, because those who want to be very modern, instead of living, fret endlessly.
