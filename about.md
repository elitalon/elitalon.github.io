## Affiliations

The opinions expressed in this weblog are my own, usually nobody sees them before they're published, and neither my employer nor any other party necessarily agrees with them.

That said, it's only fair that you know who's paying the person writing what you're reading.

I'm currently employed at [FAIRTIQ](https://fairtiq.com), working as a senior backend and iOS developer on [our iOS apps](https://apps.apple.com/developer/fairtiq-ag/id1094360402) and the [revamped Rejsekort service](https://www.rejsekort.dk/) in Denmark.

Over the years I've also helped create [Cloquo](https://web.archive.org/web/20140712053035/https://my.cloquo.com/), [Fintonic](https://www.fintonic.com/), [Sandbox](http://sandbox.is), [Numbrs](https://web.archive.org/web/20160527014416/https://www.centralway.com/de/), [GPX](https://web.archive.org/web/20170903231051/https://www.fifatms.com/gpx/) and [SBB Mobile](https://itunes.apple.com/app/sbb-mobile/id294855237), among other products.

## Privacy and security

This site is static HTML built using [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and a custom theme largely ~~inspired by~~ copied from Mark Otto's [Lanyon](https://github.com/poole/lanyon).

All the code sits in [Github](https://github.com/elitalon/elitalon.github.io) and [Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/elitalon/elitalon.github.io), and the site is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://docs.github.com/pages). I'm in the process of migrating the hosting over to [Codeberg Pages](https://docs.codeberg.org/codeberg-pages/), but I'm waiting until their new system is ready.

I don’t run any analytics software and no deliberate attempt is made by me to track you with cookies or any other technology. I never look at the logs because I don't have access to them, but I would assume my hosting provider keeps some information about your visit.
